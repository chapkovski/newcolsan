At stage 3 you can take the similar decision that you made at Stage 2, but regarding your own group member who participated in Stage 1.

You can send the deduction tokens to a member of your group who participated in Stage 1. Each deduction token sent by you to a member of your group will decrease his or her payoff by {{Constants.punishment_factor}} points while decreasing your payoff by one point. Other members of your group can do the same.
