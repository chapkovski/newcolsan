Stage 2:

In stage 2 you will be shown the decisions of another pair: one member of your own group (Participant A) and a person from group B with whom Participant A was matched in Stage 1 (Participant B). For example:

Participant from your group (A) chose Red
A partner from another group (B) with whom he or she was matched chose Blue

At the beginning of stage 2 all participants receive an additional endowment of 15 points. By transferring points from this endowment you can decrease incomes of other participants an income of a participant from Group B. For example if you send one deduction token to a Participant A, your endowment will be decreased by 1 point, but a payoff of Participant A will be decreased by 3 points. If you send 1 deduction point to a Participant B (a member of group B who was matched with Participant A), we will choose randomly one member of a group B and we will decrease his or her income by 3 points. It is not known who exactly from a group B will be picked to have his or her income diminished by your deduction tokens. If you send 1 deduction point to a Participant B (a member of group B who was matched with Participant A), your endowment will be decreased by 1 point, but a payoff of Participant B will be decreased by 3 points.

Other members of your group can make the same decisions. The members of group B also make the same decisions about sending deduction tokens to a random member of your group and/or to the specific member of their group.

When you take your decision in Stage 2 and click ‘Next’ you will be shown your and your partner’s decisions made in Stage 1 and your payoff for this round.
