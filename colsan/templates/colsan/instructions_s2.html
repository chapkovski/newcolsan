Stage 2:

In Stage 2 you see the decisions of another pair: one random member of your own group (Participant A) and a person from group B with whom he or she was matched (Participant B). {% if session.config.outgroup %}

At the beginning of Stage 2 all participants receive an additional endowment of {{Constants.punishment_endowment}} points. By transferring points from this endowment you can decrease {% if session.config.ingroup %} incomes of other participants. {% else %} an income of a participant from Group B. {% endif%} {% if session.config.ingroup %}

For example if you send 1 deduction token to a participant from your own group (Participant A), your endowment will be decreased by 1 point, but a payoff of this specific Participant A will be decreased by {{Constants.punishment_factor}} points. {% endif%} {% if session.config.colsan %}

If you send 1 deduction point to a Participant B, we will choose randomly one member of a group B and we will decrease his or her income by {{Constants.punishment_factor}} points. It is not known who exactly from a group B will be picked to have his or her income diminished by your deduction tokens. {% else %} If you send 1 deduction point to a Participant B (a member of group B who was matched with Participant A), your endowment will be decreased by 1 point, but a payoff of Participant B will be decreased by {{Constants.punishment_factor}} points. {% endif%}

Other members of your group can make the same decisions.

The members of group B also make the same decisions about sending deduction tokens to {% if session.config.colsan %} a random member of your group{% else %} the member of your group, whose decision was shown to them{% endif%}{% if session.config.ingroup %}, and/or to the specific member of their own group{% endif%}.

{% endif%} When you {% if session.config.outgroup %} make your decision in Stage 2 and {% endif%} click ‘Next’ you will be shown your and your partner’s decisions made in Stage 1 and your payoff for this round.
